DOM SQUARE TIME TRAP

Hello Chosen One,

You do not know me yet but I will be your guide in this adventure. To start the game click on the red marker on the map. Then I will brief you on your quest. After our encounter another red marker will appear on the map. You will need to press this red marker, so that you can engage with the characters in the game and pursue your adventure. The marker will turn green when you have completed this part of the quest. Once you have completed one part of the quest, make your way to the next red marker. If you want to learn more information about the characters, you can do so by pressing the “More information” on the pop-over or at the end of your dialogue with the game's characters.

Good luck Chosen One!

LIVING PASTS PROTOTYPE

Introduction to our web-based treasure hunt

Welcome to our web-based treasure hunt! Here you'll find an instruction to play the game and the link to the game itself. We created this game in order to explore the Dom Square and its surroundings from another perspective. At the same time you'll get to know several historical characters and hopefully gain some local Utrecht history along the way! On this site you can also see our trailer, look at our disclaimer and our acknowledgements. You can also take a look at our used sources on this website. This game is part of Living Past, a multidisciplinary course at Utrecht University.
